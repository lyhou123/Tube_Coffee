About Us

TUBE COFFEE created by 3 Cambodian Shareholders. Tube is inspired by London’s metro system, the underground TUBE, for which its fast speed, reliable quality, and affordability are world-renowned. Naturally, these are the very essence which Tube coffee strives to be. We aim to bring fast, affordable, and quality services to Cambodia just like our underground counterpart, but in a coffee way. As synonymous as the tube is with London, Tube coffee aims to be the same in Cambodia, simply by being a Khmer brand created in Cambodia and made by Cambodians to compete with foreign brands. By using yellow, we hope to present to the world the warmth, freshness, happiness, optimism, and positivity of the Khmer culture. So, hop aboard and enjoy a smooth ride on the Khmer tube.
